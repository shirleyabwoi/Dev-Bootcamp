JAPANS CULTURE

Did you know that Japan's cultural heritage is as diverse as its landscape?

From traditional tea ceremobies and calligraphy to cutting-edge technology and futuristic design,
Japan seamlessly blends ancient traditions with modern innovations.

Respect and harmony are central to Japanese culture

HANAMI

In this occassion people gather under Sakura trees in spring to admire their fleeting beauty.

TANABATA

Based on an ancient legend, people write wishes on colorful paper strips and hang them on bamboo trees.

OBON

A time for families to honor ancestors, often marked by lantern-lit processions.

GION MATSURI

One of Japan's biggest festivals, held in Kyoto, featuring grand parades and traditional performances.
