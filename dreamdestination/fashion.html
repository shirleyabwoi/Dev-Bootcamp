JAPANS FASHION

Japanese fashion is a fascinating mix of past and future,

from traditional kimonos to bold street styles and high-end designer brands

Whether embracing timeless elegance or avant-garde creativity,

Japan's influence on the global fashion industry remains strong.

Popular wear include the Kimono (着物), Hakama (袴) and Geta & Zori (下駄・草履)

The **kimono** is Japan's most iconic traditional garment, worn for ceremonies, festivals, and special occasions.

Hakama is a pleated skirt-like garment historically worn by samurai and students.

Geta and Zori are their traditional footwear
